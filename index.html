About the Workshop

Built for Demanding Industrial Work

Established in 1994, Micro Engineering Workshop has grown into a reliable industrial support and engineering solution provider in Bangladesh. The company manufactures high-quality import-substitute spare parts, precision components, and machinery tailored to client requirements.

The workshop supports production plants with fabrication, maintenance, repair, casting, welding, restoration, and custom engineering solutions. Its work is guided by technical expertise, skilled manpower, and a practical understanding of industrial needs.

Micro Engineering Workshop focuses on long-lasting client relationships built through reliability, consistent performance, and the ability to solve machinery challenges with locally manufactured solutions.
